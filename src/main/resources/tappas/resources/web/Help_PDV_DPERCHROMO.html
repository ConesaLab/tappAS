Project Data Visualization: Transcripts, Proteins, and Genes per Chromosome

The Transcripts, Proteins, and Genes per Chromosome chart displays the distribution of transcripts, proteins, and genes per chromosome. The chromosomes are shown on the x-axis. The corresponding counts for a given chromosome are determined by the height of the bars along the y-axis.

Don't forget to take advantage of mouseover and context-sensitive menus for additional information and functionality.
